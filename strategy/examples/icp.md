# ComplianceOS — Ideal Customer Profile

## Firmographics

| Dimension | Criteria |
|-----------|----------|
| **Employee count** | 200-5,000 |
| **Revenue** | $20M-$500M |
| **Primary industries** | Financial services, healthcare, technology (SaaS), professional services, insurance |
| **Geography** | US-primary, expanding to UK/EU |
| **Compliance maturity** | Managing 2+ regulatory frameworks simultaneously |
| **Current tooling** | Outgrown first compliance tool (Vanta/Drata) OR still running on spreadsheets |

## Dream Customer Profile

A 1,200-person financial services or healthcare company managing SOX + SOC 2 + one additional framework (HIPAA, GDPR, or ISO 27001) with a 3-6 person compliance team. They've hit the wall where:

- Audit prep consistently takes 8-14 weeks
- Each framework lives in a different tool or spreadsheet
- They've hired analysts to compensate for process gaps (not strategic growth)
- The board or executive team is asking for real-time compliance visibility they can't provide
- They've evaluated or are currently using Vanta or Drata for SOC 2 but need broader coverage

They are past the "do we need compliance automation?" question and deep into the "our current approach doesn't scale" realization.

## Buying Committee

| Role | Function | Influence |
|------|----------|-----------|
| **VP of Compliance / CCO** | Economic buyer | Final sign-off. Evaluates total program impact and vendor risk. |
| **GRC Analyst / Compliance Manager** | Champion / user buyer | Drives evaluation. Lives in the tool daily. Most motivated to change. |
| **IT Security Director / CISO** | Technical influencer | Validates integrations, evidence automation, and security posture. Gate-veto on technical fit. |
| **CFO or COO** | Executive sponsor | Approves budget. Cares about audit cost reduction and operational risk. Rarely in evaluation meetings but shapes decision criteria. |

## External Signals — When to Reach Out

**Hiring signals:**
- Job postings for GRC Analyst, Compliance Manager, or VP of Compliance on LinkedIn
- Multiple compliance roles posted within a 30-day window (indicates team scaling, not backfill)
- Job descriptions mentioning specific pain terms: "multi-framework," "audit preparation," "evidence collection"

**Growth and funding signals:**
- Series B or later funding round (triggers board governance expectations)
- Revenue milestone announcements ($50M+ ARR for SaaS, expansion-stage for services)
- New office or headcount expansion announcements in regulated industries

**Market expansion signals:**
- Entering healthcare vertical (HIPAA trigger)
- Expanding to EU markets (GDPR trigger)
- Pursuing enterprise customers requiring SOC 2 or ISO 27001 attestation
- FedRAMP or government contract pursuit (compliance maturity requirement)

**Distress signals:**
- Failed audit or public compliance incident (SEC filings, news coverage)
- Regulatory enforcement action in their industry (peer pressure)
- Customer or partner requiring compliance attestation they don't yet have
- Glassdoor or LinkedIn posts from compliance team members expressing burnout or frustration

**Technology signals:**
- Current Vanta or Drata customer (visible from case studies, badges, or LinkedIn mentions) who is now past 500 employees
- Spreadsheet artifacts visible in public compliance documentation
- RFPs or vendor evaluation processes posted on procurement platforms
