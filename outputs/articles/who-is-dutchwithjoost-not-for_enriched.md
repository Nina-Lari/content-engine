### 2. Who Dutchwithjoost is NOT for — 480 words (the heart)
Five disqualifiers, each with an honest reason and, where possible, a better alternative.
1. **On a municipality-assigned track.** Refugees and certain integrators must use government-approved providers. **We are not on DUO's approved list** — check your track first. *(Proof: red-line honesty fact.)*
2. **Already at A2 and need B1 now.** The course is A1→A2; if you're already comfortable in basic conversations and need B1 today, this isn't your next step. **Soften per brief:** a beginner who will *eventually* need B1 is still a fit, because everyone passes through A1 and A2 first. A dedicated B1 course is in the works (⚠️ verify before publish). Better alternative for the already-A2 reader: a dedicated B1 option elsewhere for now.
3. **You want classroom accountability** — a teacher in the room, classmates expecting you. Real need we don't serve. Better alternative: a classroom course. *(Proof: Evidence A, the parent learner who does better in person. Anonymize fully.)*
4. **Your budget is genuinely zero.** Free and very cheap resources exist; use them. Honest, no spin. *(No price-shaming; this is a real path for a real situation.)*
5. **You only want to pass as fast as possible** and don't care about speaking after. A memorization-based course may get you there quicker. We optimize for using the language, which takes more from you up front. *(Proof: inverse of Evidence E; honest trade-off.)*
