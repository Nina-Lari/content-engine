## Opening (definition paragraph — before H2, ~90 words)
**AEO directive:** First paragraph must directly answer "How can I practice speaking Dutch alone?" in a self-contained way (featured-snippet / AI-overview target). Lead with the answer, no throat-clearing.

**Required content (adapt the brief's definition_paragraph, de-em-dashed):** The most effective way to practice Dutch speaking alone is conversation practice with an AI partner that stays at your level, so you can respond out loud anytime without anyone switching to English. Other methods that build production: shadowing (repeating after audio to fix pronunciation and rhythm), recording yourself and reviewing it, writing sentences then saying them aloud, and narrating your day in Dutch. The methods that work force you to produce the language. Watching Dutch TV or changing your phone language only builds recognition, which most stuck learners already have.
