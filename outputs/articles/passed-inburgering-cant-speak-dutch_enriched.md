### Section 5 — How to convert recognition into speaking (~320 words)

**Key argument:** Concrete path. Output before more input — produce daily, even badly. Use words in context and in real conversation, not isolated lists. Practice at your level with something that can hold a conversation and correct you. Thread the structured option via feature→benefit→proof. No pricing.

**AEO directive:** Could use a short numbered list of moves, each self-contained.

**Evidence assigned:**
- **Tier 1 — words-in-context + conversation mechanism:** Source: `product-design-evidence.md` §2–3. Lessons are scenario-based (pharmacy, appointment, meeting neighbors); a lesson completes when you've used the vocabulary in conversation with Joost, not when you've finished exercises. Joost stays at your level and only uses words you've practiced; the conversation changes based on what you say.
- **Tier 2 — words-in-context quote (verbatim, "one learner"):** *"The words that you learn, you use. If I'm using them in a sentence and I know the context, it helps me communicate."* Source: `product-design-evidence.md` §5.
- **Tier 2 — level-matched practice quote (verbatim, "one learner"):** *"Joost knows your level. He's not gonna use C2 level Dutch to reply to you."* Source: `product-design-evidence.md` §5 + value_prop #3.
- **CONCRETE CONVERSION EXAMPLE (brief flagged this as NEEDS — built from mechanism, not fabricated):** Take a word you *recognize* but never *say* — e.g., *afspraak* (appointment), a word every passer has seen. Recognition stage: you'd tick it on a multiple-choice. Production stage: today you actually use it — book a real appointment, or in the course's "making an appointment" scenario you tell Joost when you want to come, he replies at your level, and the lesson isn't marked done until you've used the words in that back-and-forth. That's the conversion from recognized to produced, made concrete. (Grounded in `product-design-evidence.md` §2–3 scenario design; no invented metric — do NOT expose "90% of vocab" / "2+ uses" implementation specifics.)
- **Feature→Benefit→Proof chain for the writer:** Feature = lesson completes only when you've used the vocab in conversation with Joost.
