### Section 4: InburgeringOnline: Where It Falls Short
**Word Target:** 200
**Evidence Needed:** Common feedback patterns

**Available Evidence:**
- Recognition vs. production gap (Seidlitz Education, citable): "Many learners can recognize Dutch vocabulary but cannot use it"
- Taalhammer blog (citable): Isolated word memorization fades without context
- Community discussions (anonymize): People report passing but still unable to converse
- Research: Karpicke & Roediger (2008) — restudying has no effect on delayed recall

**Key Argument:**
The limitation isn't InburgeringOnline specifically — it's the memorization approach. When completion means finishing exercises, you learn to recognize correct answers, not produce them. The research is clear: recognition and production are different cognitive skills.
