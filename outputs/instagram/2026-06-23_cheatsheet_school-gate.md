## SLIDES (lay out in Canva/CapCut)
2. **Is dat jouw zoon?** / **Is dat jouw dochter?**  (Is that your son? / Is that your daughter?)  ·  _when:_ opening a chat about their kid.
3. **Hoe heet hij?** / **Hoe heet zij?**  (What's his name? / What's her name?)  ·  _when:_ right after, to keep it going.
4. **Hoe oud is hij?**  (How old is he?)  ·  _when:_ easy follow-up that always works.
5. **In welke groep zit hij?**  (Which group is he in?)  ·  _when:_ finding the class connection between your kids.
6. **Wat een lieve meid.** / **Wat een vrolijke jongen.**  (What a sweet girl. / What a cheerful boy.)  ·  _when:_ a warm, low-stakes compliment.
7. **Zullen ze samen spelen?**  (Shall they play together?)  ·  _when:_ suggesting a playdate without overthinking it.
8. **Mag ik je nummer?**  (Can I have your number?)  ·  _when:_ swapping contacts to set it up.
9. **Tot morgen!**  (See you tomorrow!)  ·  _when:_ a friendly goodbye at the gate.
10 (last). **Save + send.** "Save this for tomorrow's pickup. Send it to another parent who's learning Dutch with you."
