### CAPTION (paste into Instagram)
Why you understand Dutch but freeze when you have to speak it.

Sofia understood every word the other parent said. Then her turn came and out came "sorry, English?" That gap has a name. Understanding is recognition. Saying it back is production, and most courses only ever train the first one.

You close the gap by producing the words out loud, not by watching more. One rehearsed line at a time, until it comes without thinking.
